PERSONAL STATEMENT

Ikenna Charles Okpalaeze is a highly rated lawyer working at Olaniwun Ajayi LP, a Tier 1 and leading commercial law firm in Nigeria (Award-winning African Law Firm & West African Law Firm of the Year 2025) with offices in Nigeria, London and OHADA region.

Ikenna has a track record of academic excellence, and prides himself as a decorated scholar with catalogue of academic and leadership laurels. He is a double First Class holder from his undergraduate studies and Nigerian law school. He is well read and has several academic publications to his credit.

As a versatile legal professional, his core expertise covers Project Finance, Oil and Gas, Dispute Resolution, Technology, Innovation, and Fintech law. He has advised on the full spectrum of legal and regulatory issues affecting emerging and established tech-driven businesses across Africa, with experience spanning product development, licensing, payments, data protection, and digital financial services.

In addition to his thriving legal career, Ikenna is actively seeking out opportunities to explore the sports industry. He plans to specialize in sports law and management, which he professes to be his lifelong passion. Ikenna envisions himself as a prolific sporting director and agent for top sporting organization and clubs locally and internationally. He understands the business side of sports and believes his knowledge can assist federations and clubs commercialize opportunities in the global sports industry.

Ikenna is an ardent advocate of diversity, inclusion and cultural enrichment through cross-cultural immersion. He is a willing learner and very intentional about networking, which has earned him bosom friends from different backgrounds. He values creativity and teamwork, and he will be happy to be part of a group with similar interests in sports.

He has also demonstrated exceptional leadership qualities and has participated in extracurricular activities that have helped both students and the community over time. Over the years, he has volunteered with several professional and local organizations to positively impact society. Ikenna enjoys teaching and mentoring, and his mentees have developed into a community of scholars thriving in their fields.
